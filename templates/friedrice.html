Fried Rice
4.8 rating
Indian, Dinner
60 min

Direction

  • Wash the brown rice with clean water and soak it in 2¼ cup of water for ½ an hour.
  • Add some water to the cleaned rice and put it in pressure cooker on high flames, add 1 teaspoon oil and 1 teaspoon salt to it.
  • When it starts boiling, close the lid of pressure cooker and cook it for 7-8 minutes on low flames.
  • Remove it from heat let the steam release.
  • Remove it from heat let the steam release
  • While the rice cools down, chop all the vegetables. Cook beans, cabbage for 2 minutes in microwave. It will make them half-cooked. This step is totally optional but follow it if you want your vegetables crunchy.
  • Heat the oil in a pan, add ½ of finely chopped garlic and sauté it for few seconds do not make it brown.
  • Add spring onion, sauté it for few seconds and then add onion, bell pepper and stir fry on for few seconds.
  • Add the microwave-cooked vegetables, paneer or tofu and sauté it for a few seconds.
  • Add chili sauce, soya sauce to it.
  • Mix it.
  • Add cooked brown rice.
  • Mix it and stir fry for 4-5 minutes.
  • Add chopped garlic and black pepper powder. This step is also optional if you do not like raw garlic.
  • Serve fried brown rice hot.

Ingredients

Brown rice 1 Cup
Water 2¼ cup
butter or oil 1 teaspoon
onion chopped ¼ cup
spring onions finely chopped 3-4
strand of green garlic finely chopped (optional) 1
bell pepper (capscicum)-green, yellow or red 1 cup
julienned cabbage ½ cup
French beans finely chopped ½ cup
panner or tofu pieces ½ cup
garlic cloves finely chopped 5-6
chili sauce 2 teaspoon
soya sauce 1 teaspoon
black pepper for taste (optional) 1/4 teaspoon
Salt to taste Taste According
oil (preferably sesame seed oil / olive oil) 1 teaspoon
